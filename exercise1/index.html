Tribute to Zendaya

About Zendaya

Zendaya (born 1996) is an American actress, singer, and producer. She became widely known for her role in the Disney Channel series "Shake It Up" and later gained critical acclaim for her performance in the HBO series "Euphoria". She is also recognized for her roles in the "Spider-Man" films alongside Tom Holland.
